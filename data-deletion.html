LexiNest Data Deletion Request

This page explains how users of LexiNest: Calm Word Puzzle can request deletion of data related to the app.

App name: LexiNest: Calm Word Puzzle

Package name: com.nareshgamestudio.lexinest

Developer: Naresh Game Studio

Contact email: nareshgamesstudio@gmail.com

Does LexiNest create a user account?

LexiNest does not create a separate LexiNest account with an email address, username, or password. The game may automatically attempt Google Play Games sign-in to support cloud save. If Google Play Games sign-in is not available, skipped, or fails, the game can still be played using local save data on the device.

What data can be deleted?

Depending on how you use the game, LexiNest may store game progress data such as current level, coins, hints, rewards, daily reward progress, solved words, and related game settings.

This data may be stored locally on your device and, if you use Google Play Games sign-in, may also be saved through Google Play Games cloud save.

Delete data from inside the app

In supported versions of LexiNest, you can delete your game data from inside the app:

  1. Open LexiNest.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete My Game Data.
  4. Confirm the deletion.

This will reset local game progress on your device. If you are signed in with Google Play Games and cloud save is available, the app will also attempt to delete or reset your LexiNest cloud save data.

Delete local app data manually

You can also delete local data from your Android device:

  1. Open Android Settings.
  2. Go to Apps.
  3. Select LexiNest.
  4. Open Storage.
  5. Tap Clear data or Clear storage.

You may also uninstall the app to remove local app data from your device.

Request deletion by email

You can request data deletion by emailing us at: nareshgamesstudio@gmail.com

Please use the subject: LexiNest Data Deletion Request

If you contact us by email, we may ask for additional information only if needed to understand your request. Since LexiNest does not create its own user account, we may not be able to identify Google Play Games cloud save data from email alone. In that case, we will guide you to delete or reset data from inside the app.

Support emails

If you contacted us for support, we may have your email address and message in our support mailbox. Upon request, we will delete support communication related to your deletion request, unless we are required to retain it for legal, security, or fraud prevention reasons.

Third-party services

LexiNest may use Google Play Games Services and Google AdMob. Data handled directly by Google is subject to Google's own privacy and data controls. You can manage your Google data through your Google Account settings.

Processing time

We aim to respond to deletion requests within a reasonable time, usually within 7 to 30 days.

Contact

For any privacy or deletion request, contact: nareshgamesstudio@gmail.com
