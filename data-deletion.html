LexiTide Data Deletion Request

This page explains how users of LexiTide: Calm Word Puzzle can delete or request deletion of data related to the game.

Package name: com.nareshgamestudio.lexinest

Developer: Naresh Game Studio

Does LexiTide create a user account?

LexiTide does not create a separate LexiTide account with an email address, username, or password.

The game may attempt to sign you in through Google Play Games Services to support cloud-save functionality. Depending on your device and Google Play Games settings, this sign-in may occur automatically.

If Google Play Games sign-in is unavailable, skipped, or unsuccessful, the game can continue using locally stored save data on your device.

What data can be deleted?

Depending on how you use the game, LexiTide may store gameplay information including:

  • Current level and level progress
  • Coins and hints
  • Daily reward and streak progress
  • Bonus-word progress
  • Solved words and unlocked Word Journal entries
  • Sound, vibration, notification, and gameplay settings
  • Other information required to save or restore game progress

This information may be stored locally on your device. If Google Play Games sign-in and cloud save are available, eligible gameplay progress may also be stored through Google Play Games Services.

Other applicable information may include support correspondence and pseudonymous analytics information associated with an installation of the game.

Analytics data and installation identifiers

LexiTide uses Google Analytics for Firebase to understand approximate installs, active users, sessions, engagement, device types, regions, and game-feature usage.

Google Analytics for Firebase generally associates analytics information with a pseudonymous app-instance or installation identifier rather than a separate LexiTide account.

Analytics information may include first launches, app opens, sessions, engagement, app version, device characteristics, approximate country or region, automatically collected app-lifecycle events, and limited gameplay events.

Because LexiTide does not assign your email address to its Analytics installation identifier, Naresh Game Studio generally cannot locate a particular installation's Analytics information using an email address alone.

Delete data from inside the app

In supported versions of LexiTide, you can reset your game data from inside the app:

  1. Open LexiTide.
  2. Open Settings.
  3. Tap Delete My Game Data.
  4. Review the warning and confirm the deletion.

This action resets applicable locally stored gameplay progress and settings on your device.

If you are signed in through Google Play Games and cloud save is available, the game may also attempt to reset or replace the associated LexiTide cloud save with a fresh default save.

Cloud operations may depend on your internet connection, Google Play Games sign-in status, and service availability.

Resetting gameplay progress does not necessarily delete historical, aggregated, or previously processed information held independently by third-party service providers.

Delete local app data manually

You can remove locally stored LexiTide data through your Android device settings:

  1. Open Android Settings.
  2. Open Apps or Applications.
  3. Select LexiTide.
  4. Open Storage or Storage and cache.
  5. Tap Clear data or Clear storage.

You may also uninstall LexiTide to remove locally stored app data from the device.

Clearing local data or uninstalling the game may reset identifiers used by a future installation. It may not automatically remove gameplay progress stored through Google Play Games cloud save or historical information that has already been processed by third-party services.

We may ask for limited additional information only when reasonably necessary to understand, identify, verify, or process your request.

Because LexiTide does not operate its own user-account system, we may not be able to identify Google Play Games cloud-save data or pseudonymous Analytics information using your email address alone.

Where applicable, we may provide instructions for using the available in-game, Android-device, Google Play Games, Google Account, or installation controls.

When Analytics information can reasonably be identified through an applicable installation identifier and is under the control of Naresh Game Studio, we will review the available Google Analytics deletion controls for the request.

Support emails

If you contacted Naresh Game Studio for support, your email address and message may remain in our support mailbox.

Upon request, we will delete applicable support correspondence unless it must be retained for legal compliance, security, abuse prevention, fraud prevention, or dispute resolution.

Data that may be retained

Certain information may be retained when reasonably necessary for legal compliance, security, fraud or abuse prevention, dispute resolution, or the protection of users and services.

Aggregated or de-identified reports that are no longer reasonably linked to a particular installation or individual may also be retained for statistical and historical purposes.

Third-party services

LexiTide may use third-party services including:

  • Google Play Games Services
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Installations
  • Google AdMob
  • Google User Messaging Platform
  • Google Play services
  • Unity and related development services

Information processed independently by these providers is governed by their own terms, privacy policies, retention practices, deletion processes, and account controls.

You may manage applicable Google information through your Google Account, Google Play Games settings, advertising settings, and other Google privacy controls.

Processing time

We aim to acknowledge and process valid deletion requests within a reasonable period, generally within 7 to 30 days.

Additional time may be required when identification, verification, technical limitations, legal obligations, security requirements, or third-party services affect the request.
