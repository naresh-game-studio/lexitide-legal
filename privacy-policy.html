Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

LexiTide: Calm Word Puzzle is a word puzzle game published by Naresh Game Studio. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be stored, collected, processed, and used when you use the game.

Information Stored by the Game

LexiTide does not require you to create a separate LexiTide account with an email address, username, or password.

The game may store gameplay information locally on your device, including:

  • Current level and completed levels
  • Coins and hints
  • Daily reward and streak information
  • Bonus-word progress
  • Unlocked Word Journal entries
  • Sound, vibration, notification, and gameplay settings
  • Other information required to save and restore game progress

Google Play Games Sign-In and Cloud Save

LexiTide may use Google Play Games Services to provide sign-in and cloud-save functionality. Depending on your device and Google Play Games settings, the game may attempt to sign you in automatically.

Google Play Games sign-in is used to support game functionality such as saving, synchronising, and restoring gameplay progress across supported devices.

When Google Play Games sign-in is available, gameplay information such as your current level, coins, hints, bonus-word progress, daily reward or streak status, settings, and save-update information may be stored through Google Play Games Services.

If Google Play Games sign-in is unavailable, skipped, or unsuccessful, LexiTide may continue using locally stored game progress.

Naresh Game Studio does not receive your Google account password. Your use of Google Play Games Services is also subject to Google's applicable terms and privacy policies.

Analytics

LexiTide uses Google Analytics for Firebase to understand how the game is installed and used, measure game performance and engagement, and improve the player experience.

Google Analytics for Firebase may automatically collect or process information including:

  • First launches, app opens, and app updates
  • Sessions, session duration, and engagement information
  • Operating-system and app-version information
  • Device brand, model, language, and general device characteristics
  • Approximate country or geographic region
  • An app-instance identifier or Firebase installation identifier used to distinguish installations of the game
  • The Android Advertising ID where it is available and permitted by device, account, consent, and applicable privacy settings
  • Automatically collected app-lifecycle events and related technical information

LexiTide may also record limited gameplay events to understand how features are used. These may include:

  • Starting or completing a level
  • Using a hint
  • Completing a rewarded advertisement
  • Displaying an interstitial advertisement
  • Finding a bonus word
  • Claiming a daily reward
  • Opening the Word Journal

Gameplay analytics may include limited parameters such as a level number, event result, reward type, or feature source. LexiTide does not intentionally send your name, email address, Google account password, or the contents of personal communications as analytics event parameters.

LexiTide does not currently assign a separate LexiTide user ID to Google Analytics. Analytics information is generally associated with a pseudonymous app installation identifier rather than a separate LexiTide account.

Analytics information is used to understand approximate installs, active users, sessions, engagement, devices, regions, and game-feature usage, and to maintain and improve the game.

Advertising

LexiTide may display advertising, including rewarded and interstitial advertisements. Advertising may be provided by third-party services such as Google AdMob.

Advertising providers may collect or process information such as device information, advertising identifiers, approximate location, app activity, ad interactions, crash logs, diagnostics, and related technical information to provide, measure, protect, personalise where permitted, and improve advertising and app performance.

Where required, LexiTide may show a consent message that allows you to review or manage privacy and advertising choices. You may also be able to change those choices later through the privacy options available in the game.

Notifications

LexiTide may request permission to send reminder notifications. Notifications may remind you to return to the game and continue solving word puzzles.

You can enable or disable notifications through the game settings or your device settings.

Children's Privacy

LexiTide is intended for a general audience. Naresh Game Studio does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children through a separate LexiTide account.

Third-party services used by the game may process information according to their own terms, age requirements, consent requirements, and privacy policies.

Data Security

Reasonable technical and organisational measures are used to protect information handled through the game. Information transmitted to supported Firebase services is encrypted in transit.

No electronic transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Data Storage and Retention

Local game progress and settings remain on your device until they are deleted by the game, removed through device or app settings, or removed when the application is uninstalled.

If Google Play Games cloud save is used, certain gameplay information may remain associated with your Google Play Games account until it is overwritten or deleted through the available deletion process.

Analytics information is retained according to the Google Analytics retention settings selected by Naresh Game Studio and Google's applicable service policies. Information may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to measure usage, maintain the service, improve the game, meet legal obligations, prevent abuse, or resolve technical issues.

Aggregated or de-identified reports that are no longer reasonably linked to a particular installation may be retained for statistical and historical purposes.

Data Deletion

LexiTide includes an in-game option to delete locally stored game progress and reset the game.

Instructions for requesting or completing deletion of applicable LexiTide game data are available on the following page:

LexiTide Data Deletion

Firebase Analytics information is generally associated with a pseudonymous installation identifier rather than a LexiTide account. You may contact Naresh Game Studio to request deletion of applicable information that can reasonably be identified and controlled by Naresh Game Studio.

Certain aggregated, de-identified, legally required, fraud-prevention, or security-related information may not be capable of being associated with a particular person or may need to be retained where permitted by law.

Third-Party Services

LexiTide may use third-party services including:

  • Google Play Games Services
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Installations
  • Google AdMob
  • Google User Messaging Platform
  • Google Play services
  • Unity and related development services

These services may process information according to their own terms and privacy policies. Naresh Game Studio does not control the independent privacy practices of third-party service providers.
