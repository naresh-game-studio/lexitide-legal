Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

LexiNest is a word puzzle game published by Naresh Game Studio. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our game.

Information We Collect

LexiNest does not require users to create an account. We may store basic game progress locally on your device, such as level progress, coins, hints, settings, daily reward status, bonus word progress, and gameplay preferences.

Google Play Games Sign-In and Cloud Save

LexiNest may offer optional sign-in with Google Play Games. This feature is used to support game functionality such as saving and restoring gameplay progress across devices.

If you choose to sign in with Google Play Games, LexiNest may use Google Play Games Services to save gameplay progress such as your current level, coins, hints, bonus word progress, daily reward or streak status, and basic game settings.

This information is used only for app functionality, including saving your progress and restoring it when you reinstall the app or use another supported device.

LexiNest does not create its own user accounts, and Google Play Games sign-in is optional. Your use of Google Play Games is also subject to Google's applicable terms and privacy policy.

Advertising

LexiNest may show ads, including rewarded ads. Ads may be provided by third-party advertising partners such as Google AdMob. These partners may collect or use device information, advertising identifiers, approximate location, app activity, crash logs, diagnostics, and related information to provide, measure, protect, and improve advertising and app performance.

Notifications

LexiNest may ask for permission to send reminder notifications. You can enable or disable notifications from the game settings or your device settings.

Children's Privacy

LexiNest is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Storage

Game progress and settings may be stored locally on your device using local storage mechanisms. If you uninstall the game, this local data may be removed.

If you choose to use Google Play Games sign-in, certain gameplay progress may also be saved using Google Play Games Services to help restore your progress.

Third-Party Services

LexiNest may use third-party services such as Google AdMob, Google Play Games Services, and Unity-related services. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
